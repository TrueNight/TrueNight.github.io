Shopping app for customers and app for shop owners to create your own shop and add products to it.

‍

Customer app features:

  • Registration / authorisation
  • Catalog of products with filters and sort
  • Product card
  • Favourites
  • Cart with online payment
  • User profile with delivery info
  • Chat
  • Notifications

Seller app features:

  • Registration / authorisation
  • Add new product
  • My products list
  • Shop's analytics
  • Order list
  • Chat
  • Shop profile
