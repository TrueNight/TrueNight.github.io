Customer app for Butik dark store, which can interact with Fit-Assist (application in the fitting room) and Sale-Assist (consultant application).

App features:

  • Catalog of products with filters and sort
  • Product card with 3d photo, similar items, look on model and more
  • Wishlist
  • Fitting list (to order inside offline shop's lounge in 1 click)
  • Cart with online payment
  • User profile
  • Defining of delivery address by geo
  • Different UI/UX when user in/out of offline shop
  • Notifications

Shared features:

  • Fitting list (also in Fit-Assist, Sale-Assist)
  • Pick-up order list (also in Fit-Assist, Sale-Assist)
  • Notification for authoriztion on Fit-Assist
