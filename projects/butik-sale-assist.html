App for consultants in Butik dark store, which can interact with Butik Customer App, Fit-Assist (fitting room application) and Fitting-Queue app.

My responsibilities as project tech lead and architect:

  • Development from scratch
  • Development of architecture for app and interaction with server
  • Coordination of development
  • Transformation of business requests to developer tasks
  • Decomposition of tasks for developers
  • Selection of devices and parts for COSU app in fitting room and hall

App features:

  • Catalog of products with filters and sort
  • Search products
  • Product card with stocks
  • Management of customer's shopping cart
  • Order product to hall or to fitting room
  • Order pick-up to hall or to fitting room
  • Cart with status of items
  • Management of fitting rooms and reservations in queue for fitting rooms
  • Barcode scanning of products
  • Assembly of goods on hall
  • Receive consultant calls
  • Notifications about room inactivity

Shared features:

  • Fitting list (also in Customer App, Fit-Assist)
  • Pick-up order list (also in Customer App, Fit-Assist)
