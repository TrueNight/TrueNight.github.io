App for business networking & contact management. With 100AM you are in the know! All vital event information is at your fingertips, as well as a contact management tool and other networking opportunities.

‍My responsibilities as project architect and mobile team lead:

  • Development of architecture for interaction between clients and server
  • Coordination of development
  • Transformation of business requests to developer tasks
  • Decomposition of tasks for developers

App's features:

  • Scan paper business cards
  • Layout of app's interface from server
  • Create digital business card with ready to use template or with own image, video or gif
  • Import contacts from Gmail (contacts list and interlocutors), Facebook, Outlook, Phone contacts
  • Registration/Sign-In
  • Subscriptions and in-app purchases
  • Contacts list
  • Watch contact (image/video/gif content)
  • Add notes to contacts
  • Set To-Dos and sync them with calendar for each contact
  • Sync contacts from 100AM with phone’s address book
  • Sync contacts from phone's address book to 100AM
  • Groups (local - visible only to user; shared - visible to all members of group, can be public or private; corporate - group of the company)
  • Events (public, private) with schedule, floor plan, members, updates
  • Event stats for owner
  • Meetings (invite user to meeting inside event, reschedule meeting, approve/cancel requested meeting)
  • Import of event from Eventbrite or Timepad
  • Share digital business card via NFC, QR, link, vCard, BLE
  • Find contacts nearby (BLE, Geolocation)
  • Generate image of business card with embedded QR
  • Notifications about changes in contacts, groups and events
  • Search contacts by info, tags, phone, email and etc.
