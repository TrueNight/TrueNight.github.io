Pet project for public transportation tracking and displaying of bus arrival prediction. Data of public transport's current locations for this app was provided by goverment of Tomsk.

App contains:

  • Current location for public transport items on map
  • Routes for public transportation and location of transport for specific route
  • Stops on map with prediction of transport arrival time to this stop
  • Search of route/stop
  • Add route to favourites
  • Add stop to favourites
  • Notifications (remind about transport is coming to bus stop by time or distance)
