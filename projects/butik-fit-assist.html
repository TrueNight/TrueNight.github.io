App for fitting rooms in Butik dark store, which can interact with Butik Customer App, Sale-Assist (consultant application) and Fitting-Queue app.

‍

My responsibilities as project tech lead and architect:

  • Development from scratch
  • Development of architecture for app and interaction with server
  • Coordination of development
  • Transformation of business requests to developer tasks
  • Decomposition of tasks for developers
  • Selection of devices and parts for COSU app in fitting room and hall

App features:

  • Supports 10", 15", 25", 32" touch displays (Full HD and 4k)
  • Catalog of products with filters and sort
  • Product card with similar items, look on model and more
  • Order product or pickup to fitting room
  • Fitting list (order fitting list from Customer App)
  • Cart with status of ordered items (ordered, in fitting room and etc.)
  • User sign-in for auto-apply discounts and import of fitting list and pickup orders
  • Barcode scanning of products
  • Call consultant button

Shared features:

  • Authorisation (also in Customer App)
  • Fitting list (also in Customer App, Sale-Assist)
  • Pick-up order list (also in Customer App, Sale-Assist)
