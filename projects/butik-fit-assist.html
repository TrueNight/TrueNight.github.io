App features:

  • Supports 10", 15", 25", 32" touch displays (Full HD and 4k)
  • Catalog of products with filters and sort
  • Product card with similar items, look on model and more
  • Order product or pickup to fitting room

  • Cart with status of ordered items (ordered, in fitting room and etc.)
  • User sign-in for auto-apply discounts and import of fitting list and pickup orders
  • Barcode scanning of products
  • Call consultant button
